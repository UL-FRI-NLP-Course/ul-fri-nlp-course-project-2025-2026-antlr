Opis

I am a research assistant and a PhD student at the Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana, Slovenia. My research interests lie in the field of human-computer interaction, wearable sensing, electrophysiological signal processing and analysis, and machine learning. My current research focuses on deep learning applications for the automated assessment of physiological and cognitive states.

I have five years of industry experience, working in the research and development of electroencephalography (EEG) systems and in the automotive industry, specifically with system durability and performance tests conducted on electric drive axles.
