Opis

I am an **associate professor** at the **Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana, Slovenia**. My research is focused on mobile deep learning, approximate computing, and resource-efficient computing.

NEWS

[Attractive postdoc opportunity in mobile AI](https://smash.ung.si/open-calls/) with the application deadline on October 21st!

I am looking for motivated PhD students! I have a number of active and potential projects on federated learning, compressive sensing for mobiles, and approximate mobile computing. The University of Ljubljana, Slovenia is one of the top research universities in the region, offers a range of teaching and research fellowships and is located in one of the most beautiful parts of Europe. Please email me (Veljko.Pejovic [at] fri.uni-lj.si) if you want to chat about PhD studies here.

In addition, please get in contact if you are interested in doing your bachelor or masters thesis under my supervision in year 2023/2024.
